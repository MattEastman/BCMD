Get Better Care In Three Simple Steps

A path to wellness starts with a first step, we can help.
1. CALL TO SCHEDULE A VISIT

Call us at 1-866-888-CARE (2273), complete the online form or download BettercareMD™ app to get matched with a caring doctor.

You can complete the online request form below and one of our health navigators will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a visit with a doctor.

Or call 1-866-888-CARE (2273), to speak to a health navigator who will connect you with a qualified doctor in your state within minutes. You can also download the BettercareMD™ app to schedule a visit and connect with a doctor. We accept most health plans and offer self-pay options too.

Complete Form
2. TALK TO A DOCTOR

Your doctor will listen to your health concerns and history then make a diagnosis.

Once we find a match for you, the doctor will ask you a few questions about past conditions, medications, allergies and any information about your family’s medical history.This information is gathered via internet video or phone to help the doctor assess your symptoms and make a diagnosis.

Our doctors treat conditions like Cold and Flu symptoms, Bronchitis, Acne, Ear infection, Conjunctivitis, Fever, Allergies, Pink eye, Skin infection, Insomnia, Sinusitus and more. Our doctors also offer treatment advice on how to prevent and manage chronic conditions such as Diabetes, Obesity, Heart Disease and other serious health issues.

3. CHOOSE TREATMENT OPTION

Your doctor will recommend treatment options and you get to ask questions in order to make an informed decision.

The doctor will recommend treatment options and you get to ask questions in order to make an informed decision. A doctor may write a prescription for medication if medically necessary and send it to the pharmacy of your choice. You may have the option of getting your medications mailed to your home. Our doctors are team players and coordinate care with your primary care physician so they would be glad to share the results of your visit with your doctor if you approve.
